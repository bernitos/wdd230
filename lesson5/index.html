Marifa Bio center

Built on an area of ​​7 hectares, the Fama Bio center improperly called Maféré zoo was founded in 2018. Housed in the middle of the forest, it is more and more the object of curiosity with its ponds,is There are rather rare species there which are a favorable ground for research. Parked in enclosures carefully maintained by professionals and lovers of animals and nature. There are saurians, ostriches, Australian emus, giant Galápagos tortoises or camels, horses, donkeys, antelopes, monkeys, geese.There is practiced raniculture or frog farming, snail farming, pigeon farming, grass-cutter farming, poultry farming with breeds such as the Brahma and the orpington, which are chickens that can reach in their growth up to a meter span.

Species that are the proponent's choice reflect the richness and variety. Naturally to break with deja vu. Several ponds have been created for fish farming. His organic label, he claims it. Everything is produced locally. Waste produced by animals is composted to produce earthworms. A production of 250 kg per year.Activities are also planned for adult visitors, young people and children. A hike on the hills is organized for those who wish with two circuits, one of which is 1 km 5 short and another 3 km long. A FIFA-standard football pitch is under construction. A play area is under construction to allow children to properly furnish the time of their presence on the site. Its restaurant at the edge of its natural swimming pool offers succulent dishes. Moreover, everything cooked there is produced on site, which reassures customers. The Fama bio center seduces with the tranquility it offers to all those who cross its gate.
